# PERFECT PROMPT – EXAM PREPARATION FROM NOTES / SCREENSHOTS

I am preparing for university examinations.

I will upload one or more of the following:

* Lecture notes
* Handwritten notes
* PowerPoint slides
* Tutorial sheets
* Assignment questions
* Past papers
* Screenshots of lecture content
* Lab sheets
* Reference material

Your task is to act as an expert lecturer, examiner, and teaching assistant simultaneously.

---

## PRIMARY OBJECTIVE

Analyze every uploaded page carefully and create a complete exam-oriented study presentation.

The goal is NOT merely to summarize the notes.

The goal is to identify:

1. What concepts are being taught
2. Why they are important
3. How they can appear in examinations
4. Common mistakes students make
5. Hidden assumptions
6. Frequently tested derivations
7. Expected proof-based questions
8. Numerical problem-solving techniques
9. Conceptual traps
10. Examiner thinking patterns

---

## OUTPUT FORMAT REQUIREMENT

Generate ONLY LaTeX Beamer code.

Do NOT provide explanations outside the LaTeX code.

The output must compile directly.

Use:

```latex

```

Include:

* Professional theme
* Table of contents
* Colored boxes
* Mathematical equations
* Figures (TikZ when appropriate)
* Summary slides
* Revision slides
* Exam strategy slides
